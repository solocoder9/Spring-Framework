+-------------------+---------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
| Feature           |             @ModelAttribute           |               @RequestParam             |
+-------------------+---------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
| **Purpose**       | Binds entire form/command objects     | Binds individual parameters             |
+-------------------+---------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
| **Usage**         | For complex objects                   | For simple mappings (query/form params) |
+-------------------+---------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
| **Binding Scope** | Java object (command object)          | Single method parameter                 |
+-------------------+---------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
| **Validation**    | Integrated with validation frameworks | Requires manual validation              |
|                   | like JSR-303                          |                                         |
+-------------------+---------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
